## Creating Your Account

Getting started with KitchenXpert is quick and easy:

1. **Visit** [https://kitchenxpert.com](https://kitchenxpert.com)
2. **Click** the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner
3. **Choose** your registration method:
   - Email and password
   - Sign in with Google
   - Sign in with Facebook
4. **Fill in** your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, including one number and one special character)
   - Country/Region
5. **Accept** the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
6. **Click** "Create Account"

[Screenshot: Account creation form]

**Tips:**
- Use a strong, unique password for your account
- Choose an email you check regularly for important updates
- Your country selection helps us show relevant products and pricing

## Verifying Your Email

After creating your account, you'll need to verify your email address:

1. **Check** your inbox for an email from noreply@kitchenxpert.com
2. **Open** the email with subject "Verify Your KitchenXpert Account"
3. **Click** the "Verify Email" button (valid for 24 hours)
4. You'll be redirected to the KitchenXpert dashboard

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@kitchenxpert.com to your contacts
- Click "Resend Verification Email" on the login page
- Wait a few minutes before requesting another email

## Creating Your First Kitchen Design

You have three ways to start designing your dream kitchen:

### Option 1: Start from Scratch (Blank Canvas)

Perfect for users who have a clear vision and want full creative control.

1. **Click** "New Blank Design" on the dashboard
2. **Set your kitchen dimensions**:
   - Width: Enter in cm (e.g., 400)
   - Depth: Enter in cm (e.g., 300)
   - Height: Default 240cm (adjustable)
3. **Choose** your room shape:
   - Rectangular
   - L-shaped
   - U-shaped
   - Custom polygon
4. **Add** doors and windows if needed
5. **Click** "Create" to open the 3D designer

[Screenshot: Kitchen dimensions setup dialog]

**Best for:**
- Experienced designers
- Users with exact measurements
- Those who want to explore freely

---

### Option 2: Use AI Questionnaire

Let our AI guide you through the design process with intelligent questions.

1. **Click** "AI Questionnaire" on the dashboard
2. **Answer** 15-20 questions across categories:

   **Kitchen Dimensions** (3 questions)
   - What are your kitchen dimensions?
   - Ceiling height?
   - Are there fixed elements (columns, pipes)?

   **Cooking Habits** (4 questions)
   - How often do you cook?
   - How many people do you typically cook for?
   - What types of cooking do you do most?
   - Do you entertain guests frequently?

   **Storage Needs** (3 questions)
   - How much storage space do you need?
   - Do you have special storage requirements?
   - Pantry preferences?

   **Appliance Preferences** (3 questions)
   - Which appliances are essential?
   - Energy efficiency priority?
   - Brand preferences?

   **Style Preferences** (2 questions)
   - What's your preferred kitchen style?
   - Color scheme preferences?

   **Budget** (1 question)
   - What's your total budget range?

3. **Submit** and wait 5-15 seconds for AI processing
4. **Review** your generated design with 2-3 alternatives
5. **Choose** your favorite or customize further

[Screenshot: AI questionnaire interface]

**Best for:**
- First-time kitchen designers
- Users who want expert guidance
- Those unsure where to start

---

### Option 3: Import Existing Floor Plan

Already have a floor plan? Upload it and trace over it.

1. **Click** "Import Floor Plan"
2. **Upload** your image (JPEG, PNG, PDF supported)
3. **Set the scale** by marking a known dimension
4. **Trace** walls, doors, and windows
5. **Click** "Start Designing"

[Screenshot: Floor plan import with scale setting]

**Best for:**
- Renovation projects
- Architects and contractors
- Users with existing blueprints

## Using the 3D Designer

The 3D designer is where your kitchen comes to life.

### Camera Controls

Navigate your 3D space with ease:

**Mouse Controls:**
- **Left-click + Drag**: Rotate camera around kitchen
- **Right-click + Drag**: Pan camera
- **Scroll Wheel**: Zoom in/out
- **Middle-click**: Reset to default view

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**
- **Arrow Keys**: Pan camera
- **+/-**: Zoom
- **Space**: Toggle 2D/3D view
- **R**: Reset camera
- **F**: Focus on selected object

**View Presets:**
- Top View (2D plan)
- Front View
- Isometric View
- First-Person View
- Bird's Eye View

[Screenshot: Camera control panel]

### Adding Components

Add products from our extensive catalog:

1. **Open** the catalog sidebar (right panel)
2. **Browse** or search for products
3. **Click** on a product to see details
4. **Drag** the product onto your canvas
5. **Drop** it in your desired location

**Component Categories:**
- Base Cabinets
- Wall Cabinets
- Tall Cabinets
- Worktops/Countertops
- Sinks & Faucets
- Appliances (Ovens, Refrigerators, Dishwashers, Cooktops)
- Islands & Peninsulas
- Accessories (Handles, Lighting, Decorative items)

[Screenshot: Dragging component from catalog]

### Positioning and Rotation

Fine-tune component placement:

**Move Components:**
- Click and drag to reposition
- Use arrow keys for precise 1cm movements
- Hold Shift for 10cm movements

**Rotate Components:**
- Select component
- Use rotation handles (circular arrows)
- Or type exact angle in properties panel
- Snap to 45° or 90° increments (toggle in settings)

**Height Adjustment:**
- Select wall-mounted items
- Drag vertical handle
- Or enter exact height in properties panel

[Screenshot: Component transformation controls]

### Snapping and Alignment Tools

Ensure perfect placement with intelligent snapping:

**Snap Settings:**
- **Grid Snap**: Align to 5cm/10cm/25cm grid
- **Object Snap**: Snap to nearby objects
- **Wall Snap**: Auto-align to walls
- **Center Snap**: Center on walls or spaces

**Alignment Tools:**
- **Align Left/Right**: Line up multiple components
- **Distribute Evenly**: Space components equally
- **Match Heights**: Make components same height
- **Flush Alignment**: Remove gaps between components

[Screenshot: Alignment guide lines in action]

### Collision Detection

KitchenXpert automatically prevents overlapping components:

- **Red highlight**: Indicates collision/overlap
- **Yellow warning**: Tight fit (less than 2cm clearance)
- **Green checkmark**: Proper clearance

You can temporarily disable collision detection in settings for advanced users.

### Measurements and Dimensions

View accurate measurements throughout your design:

- **Auto-dimensions**: Show automatically between components
- **Manual measure**: Click two points to measure distance
- **Area calculation**: Total kitchen area and zones
- **Component details**: Width, depth, height for each item

Toggle measurement display: **M** key or View menu

[Screenshot: Kitchen with dimension lines]

### Materials and Finishes

Customize the look of your kitchen:

1. **Select** a component (cabinet, worktop, etc.)
2. **Open** Materials tab in properties panel
3. **Choose** from available options:
   - Cabinet doors: Wood, laminate, lacquer, glass
   - Worktops: Granite, quartz, marble, wood, laminate
   - Hardware: Chrome, brass, black, nickel
4. **Preview** in real-time on 3D model
5. **Save** your selections

**Color Options:**
- Pre-defined color palettes
- Custom RGB/Hex color picker
- Brand-specific finishes (IKEA colors, Schmidt ranges)

[Screenshot: Material selection panel]

### Lighting Preview

Visualize your kitchen in different lighting conditions:

**Lighting Modes:**
- **Natural Daylight**: Bright, even illumination
- **Morning Light**: Soft, warm tones
- **Evening Light**: Cooler, ambient lighting
- **Night Mode**: Under-cabinet and overhead lighting only

**Add Light Sources:**
- Ceiling lights
- Pendant lights
- Under-cabinet LED strips
- Natural light from windows

Toggle shadows and reflections for photorealistic rendering.

[Screenshot: Kitchen in different lighting modes]

### Tips and Tricks

**Efficiency Tips:**
- **Duplicate similar items**: Select and press Ctrl+D
- **Create component groups**: Select multiple items and group (Ctrl+G)
- **Use layers**: Organize complex designs with layer system
- **Save views**: Bookmark camera angles for quick access

**Design Tips:**
- **Work triangle**: Position sink, stove, and fridge in triangle (optimal efficiency)
- **Counter space**: Leave at least 60cm on either side of cooktop
- **Traffic flow**: Maintain 120cm minimum for walkways
- **Landing zones**: Plan 40cm counter space near oven and fridge

## Saving Your Design

Never lose your work:

### Auto-Save

- **Automatic** saving every 2 minutes
- **Cloud-based** storage for access anywhere
- **No manual save required** (but still available)

[Screenshot: Auto-save indicator]

### Manual Save

1. **Click** File > Save (or press Ctrl+S)
2. **Name** your project (e.g., "Kitchen Renovation 2026")
3. **Add description** (optional)
4. **Choose** save location:
   - My Projects (default)
   - Shared Projects
   - Templates
5. **Click** "Save"

### Version History

Access previous versions:

1. **Click** File > Version History
2. **Browse** automatic snapshots (saved hourly)
3. **Preview** any version
4. **Restore** if needed
5. **Compare** two versions side-by-side

Versions are kept for 90 days (Premium) or 30 days (Free).

[Screenshot: Version history panel]

## Sharing Your Design

Collaborate and get feedback:

### Share via Link

1. **Click** the Share button (top-right)
2. **Choose** permission level:
   - **View Only**: Recipients can see but not edit
   - **Comment**: Can leave notes and suggestions
   - **Edit**: Full editing permissions
3. **Generate** shareable link
4. **Copy** and send via email, messenger, etc.

**Link Options:**
- Password protect
- Expiration date
- Limit number of views

[Screenshot: Share dialog with permissions]

### Direct Collaboration

Invite specific people:

1. **Click** Share > Invite People
2. **Enter** email addresses
3. **Set** permissions per person
4. **Add** personal message
5. **Send** invitations

Collaborators can:
- View and edit in real-time
- Leave comments on specific components
- Suggest alternatives
- Chat via integrated messaging

### Export as PDF

Create a presentation-ready document:

1. **Click** File > Export > PDF
2. **Choose** what to include:
   - 3D renderings (select views)
   - Floor plan (2D top view)
   - Product list with images
   - Specifications and dimensions
   - Price breakdown
   - Notes and comments
3. **Select** quality (Standard, High, Print-Ready)
4. **Generate** and download

Perfect for:
- Sharing with contractors
- Presenting to family
- Printing for reference

## Exporting Your Design

### 3D Model Export

Export for use in other software:

**Supported Formats:**
- **OBJ**: Universal 3D format (good for rendering)
- **FBX**: Autodesk format (for 3ds Max, Maya)
- **glTF**: Web-friendly format (for AR/VR)
- **STEP**: CAD format (for technical work)

**Export Process:**
1. **Click** File > Export > 3D Model
2. **Select** format
3. **Choose** detail level (Low, Medium, High, Ultra)
4. **Include** textures and materials (optional)
5. **Export** and download

[Screenshot: 3D export options]

### Product List Export

Get a shopping list:

**Formats:**
- **Excel (.xlsx)**: Editable spreadsheet
- **CSV**: Universal format
- **PDF**: Printable list

**Included Information:**
- Product name and SKU
- Quantity needed
- Individual price
- Total price per item
- Supplier/Brand
- Product URL
- Specifications
- Thumbnail image (PDF only)

### Image Renders

Create beautiful marketing images:

1. **Click** File > Export > Image
2. **Set** resolution (1080p, 4K, 8K)
3. **Choose** view angle(s)
4. **Select** lighting preset
5. **Render** (processing time: 10 seconds to 2 minutes)
6. **Download** high-quality images

**Use Cases:**
- Social media sharing
- Portfolio building
- Inspiration boards
- Contractor presentations

[Screenshot: High-quality rendered kitchen]
