## How AI Generates Kitchen Designs

### Input Requirements

- **Minimum:** Kitchen dimensions, budget range, basic style preference
- **Recommended:** Cooking habits, household size, appliance preferences,
  storage needs, brand preferences
- **More detail = Better results**

### Generation Process (5-15 seconds)

1. **Space Analysis** (1-2s): Parse dimensions, identify constraints, calculate
   available space
2. **Layout Optimization** (2-5s): Generate layouts (L-shape, U-shape, island),
   test 100+ permutations
3. **Product Selection** (2-4s): Match appliances to habits, calculate storage,
   select within budget
4. **Style Application** (1-2s): Apply aesthetics, select materials, coordinate
   colors
5. **Alternative Generation** (2-5s): Create variations, score all, select top
   3-4
6. **Rendering** (1-3s): Generate 3D preview, create product list, calculate
   totals

## Understanding the Output

### Primary Design

**3D Model:** Fully navigable kitchen with all components placed **Product
List:** Every item with SKU, quantity, price, total cost breakdown **Design
Score** (0-100):

- Space Efficiency: /25 pts - How well design uses available space
- Budget Optimization: /25 pts - Value for money, smart spending
- Style Coherence: /25 pts - Visual harmony, consistency
- Functionality: /25 pts - Work triangle, traffic flow, ergonomics

**Score Interpretation:**

- 85-100: Excellent - Ready to implement
- 70-84: Good - Minor adjustments may improve
- 55-69: Acceptable - Review carefully
- <55: Regenerate or manual design

**Specifications:** Dimensions, work triangle measurements, counter space
totals, storage capacity, clearances

## Alternative Designs

**Why alternatives?** Multiple valid solutions exist with different trade-offs

**Alternative 1: Layout Variation**

- Different cabinet arrangement (U-shape vs. L-shape vs. Peninsula)
- Trade-offs in counter space, storage, floor space, traffic flow

**Alternative 2: Budget Allocation Variation**

- Same budget, different spending priorities
- Example: Premium appliances + budget cabinets vs. opposite
- Tailored to priorities (gourmet cook vs. design enthusiast)

**Alternative 3: Style Interpretation Variation**

- Same functional layout, different aesthetic
- Different finishes, materials, hardware, colors

## Customizing AI-Generated Designs

### Full Editing Capabilities

AI design is starting point, not final. 100% editable:

- Change any component
- Add or remove elements
- Modify materials and finishes
- Adjust layout completely

### Common Customizations

1. **Swap Products:** Replace oven brand, appliance model
2. **Change Finishes:** Different cabinet colors, worktop materials
3. **Add Elements:** Wine cooler, additional storage
4. **Remove Elements:** Delete double oven, add cabinet
5. **Modify Layout:** Change from L-shape to U-shape
6. **Adjust Budget:** Upgrade some items, downgrade others

### Smart Editing Features

AI continues helping during editing:

- Style mismatch warnings and suggestions
- Work triangle efficiency impact
- Real-time budget tracking
- Collision prevention
- Cost-saving suggestions

## Regenerating with Different Parameters

### When to Regenerate

- Don't like any alternatives
- Major requirements changed (budget, appliances)
- Want completely different approach
- Trying "what if" scenarios

**Don't regenerate for minor changes** (just edit manually)

### How to Regenerate

1. View current design → Click "Adjust Parameters"
2. Questionnaire appears with previous answers pre-filled
3. Change specific answers (budget, style, appliances, brands)
4. Click "Regenerate" (counts toward monthly limit)

### Common Changes

- **Increase/decrease budget:** See how AI spends or cuts
- **Different style:** Modern → Traditional
- **Add/remove appliances:** Double oven, wine cooler
- **Different brands:** Budget → Premium or vice versa
- **Layout preference:** Force island, open shelving

**Track all generations:** Dashboard > My AI Designs shows all designs with
parameters and scores

## Tips for Best AI Results

### Input Quality

- Accurate dimensions and fixed elements
- Honest about cooking habits and needs
- Clear priorities (what matters most)
- Use "Additional Notes" for specifics

### Interpreting Results

- Don't fixate on score (love matters more than points)
- Review all alternatives
- Understand trade-offs (more storage = less counter space)

### Iterative Approach

1. **Generation 1:** Baseline - Submit initial questionnaire
2. **Generation 2:** Refinement - Adjust based on feedback
3. **Generation 3:** Fine-tuning - Minor adjustments, near perfect

**Most users find their design in 2-3 generations**

## FAQ

**Q: How does AI know good kitchen design?** A: Trained on 50,000+ real
kitchens, professional designer input, ergonomics research, building codes.

**Q: Is AI better than human designer?** A: Different strengths. AI excels at
optimization, humans at creativity. Best approach: Combine both.

**Q: Can AI design unusual spaces?** A: Yes, but very irregular shapes may be
challenging. Review carefully and customize.

**Q: Can I trust AI product selections?** A: Products are compatible and within
budget, but swap freely based on your brand experiences.

**Q: How often is AI updated?** A: Monthly model updates with new data and
improved algorithms.
