## Right to Access (Article 15)

### What This Right Means

Data subjects can confirm processing, access their data, and receive information about processing purposes, recipients, retention, rights, sources, and automated decisions.

### How to Exercise

**Self-Service**: Account Settings > Privacy > Download My Data (24-48 hours)

**Formal Request**: Email rights@kitchenxpert.com (30 days response)

### Export Formats

JSON (technical), CSV (spreadsheet), PDF (human readable)

---

## Right to Rectification (Article 16)

### What This Right Means

Correct inaccurate data and complete incomplete data.

### Self-Service

Editable in Account Settings: name, email (verification required), phone, address, preferences

### Formal Request

For data that cannot be self-edited, submit request with supporting documentation.

---

## Right to Erasure (Article 17)

### When Erasure Applies

- Data no longer necessary
- Consent withdrawn
- Objection with no overriding legitimate grounds
- Unlawful processing
- Legal obligation

### Exceptions

Legal obligation (tax records 7 years), legal claims, public interest, archiving (anonymized)

### What Happens

**Deleted**: Account info, profile, designs, personal analytics, marketing preferences, support tickets

**Anonymized**: Order history, usage analytics, error logs

**Retained**: Legal compliance records, fraud prevention data, anonymized statistics

### Self-Service Deletion

Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account (30-day grace period)

### Timeline

Soft delete immediate, grace period 30 days, hard delete day 31, backup purge 90 days

---

## Right to Restriction (Article 18)

### When Restriction Applies

Accuracy contested, unlawful processing (user prefers restriction), user needs data for legal claims, objection pending

### How It Works

**Allowed**: Storage, processing with consent, legal claims, public interest

**Not Allowed**: Regular processing, analytics, marketing, third-party sharing

---

## Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

### Portable Data

Account data, design data (JSON + images), preferences, order history, saved products

### Not Portable

Derived data (recommendations), data about others, legitimate interest data

### How to Exercise

Account Settings > Privacy > Export My Data > Portable format (48 hours)

---

## Right to Object (Article 21)

### Marketing Objection (Absolute)

Must stop immediately, no balancing test, all channels

**Methods**: Unsubscribe link, Preference Center, Direct request

### Profiling Objection

Account Settings > Privacy > Personalization > Disable recommendations/analytics
