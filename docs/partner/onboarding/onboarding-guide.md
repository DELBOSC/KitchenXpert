## Success Metrics and KPIs

Track your performance with these key metrics:

### Product Performance Metrics

**Impressions**

- Number of times your products appear in search/browse
- **Goal:** Increase month-over-month
- **Good:** 10,000+ impressions/month for 100 products

**Click-Through Rate (CTR)**

- Percentage of impressions that lead to product page views
- **Goal:** > 5% CTR
- **Good:** 8-12% CTR

**Conversion Rate**

- Percentage of completed designs that convert to quotes/orders
- **Goal:** > 20%
- **Platform Average:** 24%
