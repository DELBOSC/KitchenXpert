- Brand name
- Model number/SKU

**Specifications:**
- Dimensions (height, width, depth) in centimeters
- Weight in kilograms
- Materials and finishes
- Color options
- Technical specifications relevant to product type

**Pricing:**
- Base price in EUR (excluding VAT)
- VAT rate applicable
- Price for each variant (if applicable)
- Shipping costs or shipping rules

**Availability:**
- Current stock quantity
- Lead time for made-to-order items
- Availability status (in stock, limited, pre-order, discontinued)

### 7.2 Product Images

**Primary Image Requirements:**
- Minimum resolution: 2000 x 2000 pixels
- Format: JPEG or PNG
- Background: White or transparent
- Product must occupy at least 80% of image area
- No watermarks, logos, or promotional text

**Additional Images:**
- Minimum of 3 images per product
- Include multiple angles (front, side, detail views)
- Lifestyle/context images encouraged
- Maximum 10 images per product

**Image Quality Standards:**
- Professional quality photography
- Accurate color representation
- Sharp focus and proper lighting
- No digital manipulation that misrepresents the product

### 7.3 Product Descriptions

Descriptions must:

a) Be accurate and not misleading;

b) Be written in clear, professional language;

c) Include all material product features and specifications;

d) Comply with applicable advertising standards;

e) Not contain inappropriate content, profanity, or offensive material;

f) Not include external links or competitor references.

### 7.4 Pricing Accuracy

Partners must ensure:

a) All prices are accurate and current;

b) Prices include clear indication of currency (EUR);

c) Any additional fees or charges are clearly disclosed;

d) Promotional prices are clearly marked with validity periods;

e) Prices comply with applicable pricing laws and regulations.

### 7.5 Category-Specific Requirements

Certain product categories have additional requirements:

**Kitchen Cabinets:**
- Construction method and materials

- Customization options available

**Appliances:**
- Energy efficiency rating (EU energy label)

**Countertops:**
- Material composition
- Edge profile options
- Maintenance requirements
- Heat and stain resistance ratings
