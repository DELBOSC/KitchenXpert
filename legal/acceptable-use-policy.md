**KitchenXpert SAS**

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

## Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP" or "Policy") governs your use of the
KitchenXpert platform, including our website, web application, mobile
applications, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

This Policy is incorporated into and forms part of the KitchenXpert
[Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service). By using the Service, you agree to
comply with this Policy. Capitalized terms not defined herein have the meanings
given in the Terms of Service.

### Purpose

The purpose of this Policy is to:

- Ensure a safe and productive environment for all users
- Protect the integrity and security of the Service
- Prevent abuse and misuse of the platform
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations

### Scope

This Policy applies to all users of the Service, including:

- Free tier users
- Paid subscribers (Pro, Business, Enterprise)
- API users and developers
- Collaborators and invited guests

Violations of this Policy may result in suspension or termination of your
account and access to the Service.

- Designing and planning personal kitchen renovation projects

### Business Use (Paid Plans)

- Designing kitchens for clients as a design professional
- Creating proposals and presentations for customers

### General Permitted Activities

- Browsing and searching the product catalog
- Saving and organizing design projects
- Using AI-powered recommendations and suggestions
- Participating in community forums and discussions
- Providing feedback and suggestions to KitchenXpert

## Prohibited Uses

The following activities are strictly prohibited when using the Service. This
list is not exhaustive; KitchenXpert reserves the right to determine what
constitutes a violation.

### Illegal Activities

You must not use the Service to:

- Engage in any activity that violates applicable laws or regulations
- Conduct or facilitate fraud, deception, or misrepresentation
- Launder money or engage in financial crimes
- Violate export control laws or sanctions
- Engage in any activity that could result in civil or criminal liability

### Harmful Content

You must not upload, create, or share content that:

- Contains malware, viruses, trojans, or other malicious code
- Facilitates phishing, social engineering, or identity theft
- Promotes or incites violence, terrorism, or self-harm
- Contains hate speech or promotes discrimination based on race, ethnicity,
  religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected
  characteristics
- Is obscene, pornographic, or sexually explicit
- Is defamatory, libelous, or harassing
- Threatens, intimidates, or bullies others
- Exploits or harms minors in any way

### Intellectual Property Violations

You must not use the Service to:

- Infringe copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property rights
- Upload or share content you do not have the right to use
- Misappropriate trade secrets or confidential information
- Violate patents or other proprietary rights
- Remove or alter copyright notices or attributions
- Claim ownership of content created by others

### System Abuse

You must not:

- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service, other accounts, or related
  systems
- Probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities without written authorization
- Conduct denial-of-service (DoS) or distributed denial-of-service (DDoS)
  attacks
- Overwhelm, flood, or spam the Service infrastructure
- Interfere with the proper functioning of the Service
- Exploit bugs, glitches, or vulnerabilities
- Circumvent security measures, access controls, or usage limits
- Use the Service in a manner that degrades performance for other users

### Automated Access and Scraping

You must not:

- Use bots, crawlers, scrapers, or automated tools without authorization
- Harvest, collect, or aggregate user data without consent
- Mirror or cache significant portions of the Service
- Systematically download or copy content from the Service
- Use automated tools to create accounts or submit requests
- Access the API in violation of rate limits or API terms

### Commercial Misuse

You must not:

- Resell, redistribute, or sublicense access to the Service
- Create a competing product using our Service or data
- Collect user information for commercial purposes without consent
- Send unsolicited commercial communications (spam) through the Service
- Use the Service to advertise or promote third-party products without
  authorization
- Exploit the Service for cryptocurrency mining or similar resource-intensive
  activities

### Platform Manipulation

You must not:

- Create fake or misleading accounts
- Create multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions or abuse promotions
- Impersonate other users, employees, or entities
- Manipulate ratings, reviews, or community features
- Game referral programs or promotional offers
- Engage in astroturfing or fake engagement

## Content Standards

When creating and sharing content through the Service, you must adhere to the
following standards:

### Design Content

- Use accurate measurements and dimensions
- Provide truthful descriptions and project names
- Respect manufacturer branding and product accuracy
- Do not create designs intended to mislead or deceive

### Project Names and Descriptions

- Use appropriate, non-offensive naming
- Do not include obscene, discriminatory, or offensive language
- Do not impersonate projects belonging to others

### Shared Content

- Only share content you have the right to share
- Respect the privacy of individuals depicted or referenced
- Do not share confidential or proprietary information without authorization
- Consider the audience when sharing publicly

### Community Participation

- Be respectful and constructive in forums and discussions
- Do not spam, troll, or disrupt conversations
- Provide accurate and helpful information
- Report violations rather than engaging negatively

## Reporting Violations

### How to Report

If you become aware of any violation of this Policy, please report it to us
immediately:

**Email:** abuse@kitchenxpert.com **Subject:** AUP Violation Report

### What to Include

When reporting a violation, please provide:

- Your contact information (optional but helpful)
- Description of the violation
- Evidence (screenshots, URLs, user information if known)
- Date and time of the incident
- Any other relevant details

### Confidentiality

All reports are treated confidentially. We will not disclose reporter
information to the violating party unless required by law or necessary to
resolve the issue.

### Investigation Process

Upon receiving a report, we will:

1. Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. Investigate the reported violation
3. Take appropriate action based on our findings
4. Notify the reporter of the outcome (where appropriate)

### No Retaliation

KitchenXpert prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports a violation in
good faith.

## Enforcement

### Investigation

KitchenXpert reserves the right to investigate any suspected violation of this
Policy. This may include:

- Reviewing account activity and content
- Analyzing system logs and usage patterns
- Contacting the user for clarification
- Cooperating with law enforcement if appropriate

### Actions for Violations

Depending on the nature and severity of the violation, KitchenXpert may take one
or more of the following actions:

**Warning:**

- For first-time minor violations
- Written notice of the violation
- Requirement to cease the prohibited activity

**Temporary Suspension:**

- For repeated minor violations or moderate violations
- Account access suspended for a specified period
- Opportunity to remedy the violation

**Content Removal:**

- Removal of violating content without notice
- User notified of removal and reason

**Permanent Termination:**

- For serious violations or repeated offenses
- Immediate and permanent termination of account
- Loss of all stored data and designs
- Prohibition from creating new accounts

**Legal Action:**

- For violations involving criminal activity or significant harm
- Referral to law enforcement authorities
- Pursuit of civil remedies

### Severity Considerations

When determining appropriate action, we consider:

- Nature and severity of the violation
- Whether the violation was intentional or negligent
- History of previous violations
- Impact on other users or the Service
- Cooperation in the investigation
- Remedial actions taken by the user

## Appeal Process

### Right to Appeal

If your account is suspended or terminated for a Policy violation, you may
appeal the decision.

### How to Appeal

Submit your appeal within 30 days of the enforcement action:

**Email:** appeals@kitchenxpert.com **Subject:** AUP Appeal - [Your Account
Email]

### Appeal Contents

Your appeal should include:

- Your account information
- Description of the enforcement action
- Explanation of why you believe the action was incorrect
- Any supporting evidence or context
- Steps you will take to prevent future violations

### Review Process

Appeals are reviewed by a different team member than the original
decision-maker. We will:

- Acknowledge receipt within 5 business days
- Review all relevant information
- Make a determination within 30 days
- Communicate the final decision to you

### Decision

The appeal decision is final. KitchenXpert reserves sole discretion in
interpreting and enforcing this Policy.
