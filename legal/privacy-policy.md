## 1. Data We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Service. This
section describes the categories of personal data we collect.

### 1.1 Account Data

When you create an account with KitchenXpert, we collect:

- **Email address**: Required for account creation, login, and communications
- **Full name**: Used for personalization and communications
- **Password**: Stored in securely hashed format (we never store plain-text
  passwords)
- **Profile photo**: Optional, used for account personalization
- **Company name**: Collected for Business and Enterprise accounts
- **Job title**: Optional, collected for business accounts
- **Phone number**: Optional, used for account recovery and support

### 1.2 Design Data

When you use our design tools, we collect:

- **Kitchen designs**: All designs you create, including layouts,
  configurations, and saved projects
- **Design history and versions**: Previous versions and modifications to your
  designs
- **Products added to designs**: Items selected from our catalog for your
  designs
- **Measurements and dimensions**: Room measurements, appliance specifications,
  and spatial data
- **Design preferences**: Style preferences, color choices, and material
  selections
- **Notes and annotations**: Comments and notes you add to designs
- **Shared design data**: Information about designs shared with collaborators

### 1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with the Service:

- **Pages visited**: Which sections and features you access
- **Features used**: Tools, functions, and capabilities you utilize
- **Time spent**: Duration of your sessions and time on specific features
- **Search queries**: Searches performed within the product catalog
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system, and version
- **Browser information**: Browser type, version, and settings
- **IP address**: Used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate location
- **Referring URLs**: How you arrived at our Service
- **Click patterns**: Interactions with interface elements

### 1.4 Payment Data

When you subscribe to paid plans, payment processing is handled by Stripe, a
PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. We collect and store:

- **Transaction ID**: Unique identifier for each payment
- **Amount and currency**: Payment amounts and currency used
- **Transaction date and time**: When payments were processed
- **Subscription tier and status**: Your current plan and subscription status
- **Billing address**: Required for tax and invoicing purposes

**Important**: We do NOT store credit card numbers, CVV codes, or full payment
card details. All sensitive payment information is processed and stored securely
by Stripe. For more information, see Stripe's Privacy Policy at
https://stripe.com/privacy.

### 1.5 Communication Data

We collect information from your communications with us:

- **Support tickets**: Questions, issues, and requests submitted to our support
  team
- **Email correspondence**: Communications sent to and from KitchenXpert
- **Feedback and surveys**: Responses to surveys, feedback forms, and reviews
- **Chat conversations**: If using in-app chat or live support features
- **Phone call recordings**: Support calls may be recorded for quality purposes
  (with notice)

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we must have a valid legal
basis to process your personal data. We rely on the following legal bases:

### 2.1 Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b))

We process certain data as necessary to perform our contract with you,
including:

- Account data to create and maintain your account
- Design data to provide the design service
- Payment data to process subscriptions
- Communications necessary for service delivery

### 2.2 Consent (Article 6(1)(a))

We process certain data only with your explicit consent, including:

- Marketing emails and promotional communications
- Optional analytics and usage tracking
- Use of your designs for AI model training and improvement
- Non-essential cookies (see Cookie Policy)

You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your account settings or
contacting us at dpo@kitchenxpert.com. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the
lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

### 2.3 Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f))

We process certain data based on our legitimate business interests, where those
interests do not override your fundamental rights:

- Security monitoring and fraud prevention
- Service improvement and bug fixes
- Analytics to understand service usage
- Protection of our legal rights

### 2.4 Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c))

We process certain data to comply with legal obligations:

- Tax records and financial reporting
- Responding to lawful legal requests
- Compliance with applicable regulations
- Fraud prevention requirements

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or
Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 7.1 Right to Access (Article 15)

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We
will provide this information in a commonly used electronic format.

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data and
completion of incomplete data.

### 7.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data in certain
circumstances, including:

- The data is no longer necessary for the purposes collected
- You withdraw consent (where consent was the legal basis)
- You object to processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds
- The data was unlawfully processed

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used,
machine-readable format and to transmit it to another controller.

### 7.5 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You have the right to request restriction of processing in certain
circumstances, such as when you contest the accuracy of data.

### 7.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests or for
direct marketing purposes.

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw your consent at any time
without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

### 7.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In France,
the supervisory authority is:

**Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertes (CNIL)** 3 Place de
Fontenoy TSA 80715 75334 Paris Cedex 07 France Website: https://www.cnil.fr
