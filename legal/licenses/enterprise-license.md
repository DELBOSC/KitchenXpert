fees, KitchenXpert grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide
license to:

a) Access and use the Platform for Customer's internal business purposes;

b) Permit Authorized Users throughout Customer's organization and its Affiliates

d) Use Platform outputs for commercial purposes, including client projects; and

unlimited Authorized Users within Customer's organization and its Affiliates.
Customer is responsible for:

- Maintaining accurate user records upon KitchenXpert's reasonable request.

## ARTICLE 4: SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

### 4.1 Uptime Guarantee

KitchenXpert guarantees Platform availability of ninety-nine point ninety-five
percent (99.95%) measured on a monthly basis ("Uptime Commitment").

**Calculation:** Uptime % = ((Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total
Minutes in Month) x 100

**Exclusions from Downtime:**

- Scheduled maintenance windows (see Section 4.2);
- Force majeure events;
- Customer-caused issues (e.g., misconfiguration, excessive API calls);
- Third-party service outages beyond KitchenXpert's control; and
- Beta or preview features.

### 4.2 Scheduled Maintenance Windows

- **Standard Maintenance:** Sundays 02:00-06:00 CET
- **Advance Notice:** Minimum seven (7) days for major maintenance
- **Maximum Duration:** Four (4) hours per maintenance window
- **Emergency Maintenance:** Best effort notice via email and status page
- **Maintenance Calendar:** Published quarterly in advance

### 4.3 Service Credits

If KitchenXpert fails to meet the Uptime Commitment, Customer is entitled to
service credits:

| Monthly Uptime | Service Credit      |
| -------------- | ------------------- |
| 99.0% - 99.95% | 15% of monthly fee  |
| 95.0% - 99.0%  | 30% of monthly fee  |
| 90.0% - 95.0%  | 50% of monthly fee  |
| Below 90.0%    | 100% of monthly fee |

**Credit Process:**

- Customer must request credits within thirty (30) days of incident;
- Request must include dates, times, and description of unavailability;
- Credits applied to subsequent invoices (no cash refunds);
- Maximum credit per month: 100% of monthly fee.

### 4.4 Priority Incident Response

| Severity Level | Description                            | Response Time | Resolution Target |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------------- | ----------------- |
| Critical       | Service completely unavailable         | 1 hour        | 4 hours           |
| High           | Major feature or function broken       | 2 hours       | 8 hours           |
| Medium         | Feature degraded, workaround available | 8 hours       | 24 hours          |
| Low            | Minor issue, question, or request      | 12 hours      | 5 business days   |

## ARTICLE 10: TERM

### 10.1 Minimum Term

The minimum Initial Term is one (1) year from the Effective Date, unless
otherwise specified in the Order Form.

### 10.2 Auto-Renewal

This Agreement automatically renews for successive one (1) year periods unless
either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days
before the end of the then-current term.

### 10.3 Termination Provisions

**Termination for Convenience:**

- Either party may terminate with one hundred eighty (180) days written notice;
- Customer remains liable for fees through the end of the then-current term.

**Termination for Cause:**

- Material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice;
- Bankruptcy, insolvency, or cessation of operations;
- Violation of applicable laws.

**Effect of Termination:**

- Access to Platform terminates at end of notice period;
- Customer may export data for sixty (60) days following termination;
- Outstanding fees immediately due;
- Confidentiality obligations survive for three (3) years.
