## 1. Subscription Cancellation

### 1.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your KitchenXpert subscription at any time through one of the
following methods:

**Self-Service Cancellation:**

1. Log in to your KitchenXpert account
2. Navigate to Account Settings
3. Select "Subscription" or "Billing"
4. Click "Cancel Subscription"
5. Confirm your cancellation

**Email Cancellation:** Send a cancellation request to billing@kitchenxpert.com
with:

- Your account email address
- Account holder name
- Reason for cancellation (optional, but helps us improve)

We will process email cancellation requests within two (2) business days and
send confirmation.

### 1.2 Effect of Cancellation

When you cancel your subscription:

**Access Continuation:**

- You retain full access to all features of your current plan until the end of
  your current billing period
- For monthly subscriptions: access continues until the end of the current month
- For annual subscriptions: access continues until the end of the annual period

**Account Status:**

- Your account will be downgraded to the Free tier at the end of the billing
  period
- You will retain access to basic features available on the Free tier
- Your designs and data remain accessible, subject to Free tier storage limits

**Data Retention:**

- Your data will be retained for thirty (30) days after subscription expiration
- During this period, you may reactivate your subscription to restore full
  access
- After thirty (30) days, data exceeding Free tier limits may be archived or
  deleted
- You may request a data export at any time during the retention period

### 1.3 No Partial Refunds for Unused Time

Cancellations do not entitle you to a refund for any unused portion of your
current billing period. Your subscription remains active until the end of the
period you have already paid for. We do not offer pro-rated refunds for early
cancellation.

## 2. Refund Policy

### 2.1 Fourteen-Day Money-Back Guarantee

KitchenXpert offers a fourteen (14) day money-back guarantee for new
subscriptions:

**Eligibility:**

- Available to first-time subscribers only
- Applies to Pro and Business tier subscriptions
- Request must be submitted within fourteen (14) days of initial subscription
  purchase
- Does not apply to subscription renewals or upgrades

**How to Request:**

1. Send an email to billing@kitchenxpert.com within fourteen (14) days of
   purchase
2. Include your account email and reason for refund request
3. We will process your request within ten (10) business days
4. Refund will be issued to your original payment method

**Processing:**

- Refunds are typically processed within five to ten (5-10) business days
- Depending on your bank or credit card provider, it may take an additional
  three to five (3-5) business days for the refund to appear in your account
- You will receive email confirmation when the refund is processed

### 2.2 Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

**After Guarantee Period:**

- Subscription fees after the fourteen (14) day money-back guarantee period
- Renewal payments (monthly or annual)

**Enterprise Contracts:**

- Enterprise tier subscriptions are governed by separate contract terms
- Refund provisions are specified in the individual Enterprise License Agreement

**Add-On Purchases:**

- One-time add-on purchases after activation or use
- Additional storage purchases after allocation
- Training sessions after delivery
- Professional services after commencement

**Account Violations:**

- Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
- Accounts suspended for fraudulent activity
- Accounts closed due to payment disputes or chargebacks

**Specific Exclusions:**

- Third-party services purchased through KitchenXpert
- Partner product purchases facilitated through the platform
- Promotional or discounted subscriptions (unless otherwise stated)

### 2.3 Exceptional Circumstances

We may consider refund requests outside our standard policy in exceptional
circumstances:

- Extended platform outages affecting your ability to use the service
- Documented technical issues that prevent normal platform use
- Billing errors or duplicate charges
- Other circumstances at KitchenXpert's discretion

To request an exceptional refund, contact billing@kitchenxpert.com with detailed
documentation of your situation.

## 3. Pro-Rata Refunds

### 3.1 KitchenXpert-Initiated Termination

If KitchenXpert terminates your subscription (other than for cause), you may be
entitled to a pro-rata refund:

**Annual Subscriptions:**

- Pro-rata refund calculated for remaining full months
- Partial months are not refunded
- Example: If terminated after seven (7) months of a twelve (12) month
  subscription, you receive a refund for five (5) months

**Calculation:** Pro-rata refund = (Remaining full months / Total months) x
Annual fee paid

### 3.2 Service Discontinuation

If KitchenXpert discontinues the platform or substantially reduces
functionality:

- You will receive at least sixty (60) days advance notice
- You will receive a full refund of any prepaid subscription fees for the period
  following discontinuation
- You will have opportunity to export your data before discontinuation

## 4. Downgrade Policy

### 4.1 Downgrading Your Subscription

You may downgrade your subscription tier at any time:

**When Downgrade Takes Effect:**

- Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle
- You retain access to your current tier features until the billing cycle ends
- No refunds are issued for the difference in tier pricing for the current
  period

**Feature Adjustments:**

- Features exclusive to your current tier will become unavailable upon downgrade
- Data and designs exceeding lower tier limits will be preserved but may become
  read-only
- You may need to reduce storage usage to comply with lower tier limits

### 4.2 From Annual to Monthly

If you wish to change from annual to monthly billing:

- The change takes effect at your next renewal date
- No refund is provided for switching mid-term
- Contact billing@kitchenxpert.com if you have questions about timing

## 5. Free Trial Policy

### 5.1 Trial Terms

KitchenXpert offers free trials to new users:

**Trial Duration:**

- Standard trial period: Fourteen (14) days
- Extended trials may be offered during promotions

**Trial Access:**

- Full access to Pro tier features during trial
- No payment required to start trial
- No automatic charges if you do not add a payment method

### 5.2 Conversion to Paid Subscription

**With Payment Method on File:**

- If you added a payment method during signup, your subscription will
  automatically convert to paid at trial end
- You will be charged for your selected plan at the trial conclusion
- To avoid charges, cancel before trial expires

**Without Payment Method:**

- If no payment method is on file, your account will convert to Free tier at
  trial end
- You will need to add payment information to continue with paid features

### 5.3 Avoiding Trial Charges

To avoid being charged after a free trial:

1. Cancel your trial subscription before the trial period ends
2. Remove your payment method from your account before trial ends
3. You will receive a reminder email three (3) days before trial expiration

## 6. Chargebacks and Disputes

### 6.1 Contact Us First

Before disputing a charge with your bank or credit card company, please contact
us at billing@kitchenxpert.com. We are committed to resolving billing issues
quickly and fairly.

**Benefits of Contacting Us:**

- Faster resolution (typically within two business days)
- Avoid potential account suspension
- Preserve your account history and data
- Maintain positive account standing

### 6.2 Chargeback Consequences

If you initiate a chargeback without first attempting to resolve the issue with
us:

**Account Suspension:**

- Your KitchenXpert account may be immediately suspended pending investigation
- Access to designs and data may be restricted during suspension

**Resolution Process:**

- We will work with your financial institution to resolve the dispute
- We will provide transaction records and relevant documentation
- Resolution may take thirty (30) to ninety (90) days depending on your bank's
  process

**Account Reinstatement:**

- If the chargeback is resolved in your favor, your account will remain closed
- If the chargeback is resolved in our favor, you may request account
  reinstatement
- Reinstatement may require payment of any outstanding balance plus a EUR 25
  administrative fee

### 6.3 Fraudulent Chargebacks

Filing a fraudulent chargeback (disputing a legitimate charge you authorized)
may result in:

- Permanent account termination
- Collection action for the disputed amount
- Reporting to fraud prevention databases
- Legal action to recover costs and damages

## 7. Currency and Payment Methods

### 7.1 Currency

All prices are displayed and charged in Euros (EUR). Refunds will be issued in
EUR. If your payment method uses a different currency:

- Your bank will convert the refund using their exchange rate
- Exchange rate fluctuations may result in a refund amount that differs slightly
  from what you originally paid in your local currency
- KitchenXpert is not responsible for exchange rate differences

### 7.2 Payment Methods

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for the purchase:

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Refunded to the card used for purchase
- **PayPal:** Refunded to your PayPal account
- **Bank Transfer:** Refunded via bank transfer to the originating account
- **Other Methods:** Refunded using the same method or an equivalent method if
  the original is unavailable
